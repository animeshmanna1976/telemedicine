Dashboard Guide

Explore Our Services

Click on any of the colorful cards to access a specific health service. Here’s a quick overview:

  • Medicine Availability: Search for medicines and check stock in nearby pharmacies.
  • Community Health Dashboard: View local health trends and data.
  • Digital Health Card (QR): Access your complete medical history in one place.
  • AI Symptom Checker: Get quick, AI-powered insights into your symptoms.
  • Doctor Appointments: Schedule and manage your appointments with healthcare professionals.
  • Women & Child Health: Specialized resources for mothers and children.
  • Mental Wellness: Access tools for stress management and mental well-being.
  • Outbreak Reporting: Report and track local disease outbreaks.
  • Community Initiatives: Join local health campaigns and events.

AI Health Assistant

Click the icon in the bottom-right corner to open the AI chat. You can ask general health questions, but remember it's not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Giving Feedback

We value your opinion! Scroll down to the "Share Your Experience" section, fill in your name and message, and click "Submit Feedback". A confirmation will appear at the top of the screen.
