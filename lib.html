Frequently Asked Questions

Common flu symptoms include fever, cough, sore throat, body aches, headaches, and fatigue. While most people recover in a week, it can cause severe illness in high-risk groups.

For most adults under 50, a routine check-up every 1-3 years is recommended. Adults over 50 should aim for annual check-ups. Your doctor may suggest a different schedule based on your personal health risks.

An **epidemic** is a disease that affects a large number of people within a specific community, population, or region. A **pandemic** is an epidemic that has spread over multiple countries or continents, affecting a significant portion of the world.
